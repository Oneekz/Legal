# Coloropolis — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 14 July 2026_

> **The short version:** Coloropolis collects nothing. It has no ads, no accounts, no analytics, and
> doesn't connect to the internet. Everything a child makes stays on the device.

## Who we are

Coloropolis is a coloring app for children, published by **Yurii Kashuba**. This policy explains how
the app handles information — and the answer is that it doesn't collect any.

## Information we collect

**None.** Coloropolis does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There is
no sign-up, no login, and no server — the app has no networking code at all.

## What stays on the device

To make the app work, the following is saved **only on your device** and is never sent anywhere:

- The pictures your child colors (their artwork gallery).
- A profile name your child types in (for example "Olivia"), so more than one child can each keep their
  own artwork. This is stored locally and is not linked to any real identity.
- Simple settings, such as the last color chosen and sound preferences.

Deleting the app removes all of it.

## Photos

If you choose to save a finished picture, the app asks permission to add that one image to your
device's Photos library. The app never reads or browses your existing photos.

## Children's privacy

Coloropolis is designed to be safe for children. Because it collects no data, has no ads, no in-app
purchases, and no links out of the app for children, it does not knowingly gather any information
from children or anyone else. It is compatible with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act
(COPPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and similar laws — which is straightforward
here, because there is nothing collected to protect.

Anything intended for a grown-up — saving or sharing a picture, and exporting artwork — sits behind a
parental gate.

## Third parties

There are no third-party advertisers, analytics providers, or SDKs of any kind. The app is built
entirely with Apple's own frameworks and contains no external code that could collect data.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
