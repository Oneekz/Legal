# Privacy Policy

**App:** Fast Chinchilla
**Effective date:** July 3, 2026

Fast Chinchilla ("the app") is a single-player card game published by
Yurii Kashuba ("we", "us"). This policy explains how the app handles
information. In short: **the app does not collect, transmit, or share any
personal data.**

## Data we collect

**None.** Fast Chinchilla does not collect, store, or transmit any personal
information about you. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics.

## Data stored on your device

The app saves your preferences and last-used game settings — such as chosen
language, card style, theme, sound and haptics toggles, difficulty, pace,
opponent count, and player color — locally on your device using Apple's standard
settings storage (UserDefaults). This information:

- never leaves your device,
- is not accessible to us or any third party,
- is removed when you delete the app.

## Network access

The app works fully offline. It does not connect to the internet, contains no
advertising, and includes no third-party tracking or analytics SDKs.

## Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.
It is suitable for all ages.

## Payments

Fast Chinchilla is a one-time paid purchase. All payment is handled by Apple
through the App Store; we never receive or store your payment details. Please
review Apple's Privacy Policy for how Apple processes purchases.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same
location and the "Effective date" above will be updated.
