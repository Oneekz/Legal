# Privacy Policy — Scripture Atlas

_Last updated: 13 July 2026_

Scripture Atlas is designed to protect your privacy completely.

## We collect nothing
Scripture Atlas does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
data. The app:

- makes **no network requests** — all maps and data are bundled and work fully offline;
- contains **no analytics, advertising, or tracking** of any kind;
- requires **no account** and asks for **no permissions** (no location, contacts, camera, or photos).

## What stays on your device
The only things the app stores are your **display preferences** — your chosen
interface language, the Bible / Extended mode toggle, and whether battles are
shown on the map — saved locally on your device (in the standard iOS preferences
store). They never leave your device and are removed if you delete the app.

## Children
Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of any age.

## Third-party data
The maps and information shown are built from openly licensed data (OpenBible.info,
Natural Earth) and public reference sources, credited inside the app under
“Sources & credits.” These providers receive no information about you — the app
never contacts them.

## Changes
If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here.
